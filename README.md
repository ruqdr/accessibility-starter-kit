# Accessibility & Inclusivity Starter Kit

Welcome to the Accessibility & Inclusivity Starter Kit! This open-source resource is designed to help business leaders and organizations implement practical, effective accessibility and inclusivity initiatives in the workplace.

## Our Mission
To change the way businesses define ability and disability, and to provide tangible tools that foster universal design and individualized workspaces. We believe that inclusive workplaces benefit everyone—enhancing innovation, productivity, and employee well-being.

## What's Inside
This kit will contain:
- **Physical Workspace Accessibility Checklist**: A detailed guide to creating barrier‑free, adjustable environments.
- **Digital Accessibility Policy Template**: A ready‑to‑adapt policy that outlines your organization’s commitment to digital inclusion.
- **Speech‑Recognition Implementation Guide**: Step‑by‑step instructions for deploying and supporting speech‑recognition software.
- **Universal‑Design Principles**: Foundational concepts that can be applied to processes, communications, and physical spaces.
- **Tools & Technology Overview**: A curated list of assistive technologies and methods for alternative data entry and communication.

## How to Use This Kit
1. **Explore** the documents in this repository.
2. **Adapt** the templates and checklists to your organization’s specific needs.
3. **Collaborate** by opening issues or pull requests to suggest improvements or share your own experiences.
4. **Spread the word**—help other leaders discover these resources.
